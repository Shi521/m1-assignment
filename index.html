About Us

Stray animals have caused various social problems for a long time. And when people can’t keep raising animals and the animals don’t have a proper place to go, they will be abandoned, causing problems for the entire society.

In our shelter, we are trying our best to promote the concept of respecting life and caring for animals, and urge the government to solve social and environmental problems caused by stray animals in a humane manner. Prevent animals from stray and abuse until there are no stray animals on the street.
