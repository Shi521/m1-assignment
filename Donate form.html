Who is qualify for on site volunteer?

Those who are 18 years of age or older who are physically and mentally healthy, have a high school vocational degree or above, love animals, love life, respect life, have love, and can provide long-term cooperation for more than 72 hours a year

What does the volunteer do?

Assist in the care of animals, assist in medical treatment, introduce and guide the park, walk the dog and organize adoption activities, and promote humanity and life education.
